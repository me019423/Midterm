Lulu and Sona

-the Note-




                                                   Lulu studied the note that had been slipped through her door. She was instantly suspicious of it, due to the meeting place and presumably the person who had delivered it.She looked at the large clock on the wall – it had just gone seven o'clock. She decided to leave her room and head over to Sona's to see what she thought of the invitation, and whether she would be going.Lulu navigated the corridors of the female dorm, passing the rooms of Fiora, Poppy, and Quinn. She wondered if they might also have been invited, though was not confident about speaking to them about it – they were all very serious, which often caused clashes with Lulu's upbeat and sometimes whimsical nature. She was, however, good friends with Sona, so she was definitely the first person to talk to that came to mind.
